🛈 CRIMSON INNOVATORS

The Crimson Innovators are a subdivision of WeCode24’s interschool coding community, officially established in the first term of 2024. Led by student leaders Tariq V.D.W and Delight M., the Crimson Innovators serve as SpineRoad’s dedicated coding club — primarily focused on guiding students through the fundamentals of programming by promoting consistency, hosting events, and presenting coding challenges. With top-level dedication and a deck of creative activities, the club is designed to support and inspire every student coder on their journey.
